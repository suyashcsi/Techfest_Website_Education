Paypal is easiest way to pay online

Note: To use PayPal as the payment gateway provider for your online course you will need to apply for a Business account with them.
When this is done you will need to follow the steps below:
1.Log in to your Create account.
2.Click "Course" from the Top Menu.
3.Click "Course Settings" on the left-hand menu.
4.Click on "Payment Gateways".
5.Select the "Add New Gateway" button.
6.Click on the "PayPal" logo.
7.Select the type of PayPal integration you would like to use by clicking on the logo.
8.Fill in the required information that appears on the screen.
9.Click the "Add Gateway" button.
10.Your PayPal integration will now appear in your Payment Gateways area
